About Me

location_on Ningbo, China

I am a PhD student at Florida State University School of Communication. I am interested in the intersection of media psychology, human-computer interaction, and computational social science. My work uses data-driven methods to explore how digital platforms, especially online games, shape user emotions and identities. I am interested in understanding why and how interactive media shapes users’ emotions.
